Brief Description:

Broad Street Ministry (BSM) is a broad-minded Christian community that practices hospitality for the homeless and works for a more just world through civic engagement. Broad Street Ministry was established in May of 2005 as a Pennsylvania 501(c)(3) not-for-profit corporation.

Challenges:

BSM is a Christian faith based organization but is open to helping anyone who walks into their doors. When re-branding this great organization, I had to create a look and feel that would speak to all religions. Being a large church BSM has different sectors which work towards a common goal. The directors wanted to highlight these areas by color coding them to better help differentiate them.

Solutions:

To help make all religions feel accepted at BSM I went with a mosaic pattern. Mosaics are iconic & universally used within many religions. In Philadelphia, BSM’s church is a beautiful landmark, which is why I made the decision for it to be the face of their new logo. This helped greatly when it came to differentiating the many sectors. I color coded the areas by using the main logo as the base.
