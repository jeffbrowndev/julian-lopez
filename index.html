ALL ABOUT ME

I have experience creating work for various mediums, such as printed marketing materials as well as digital for web & mobile designs. My design style is characterized as modern, clean and mixed with abstract elements. I place great attention on small details, strong use of a grid and a refined juxtaposition between colors and layouts.
